MONARCHY MADNESS

Stuy Students against Yellow Minions (SSAYM), 2021

Rules of the Game

  • Events will occur yearly and offer you a chance to choose an option in response to the event. How you respond will impact your army size, morale, and wealth. You will be given one event each turn.
  • Depending on the choices you make, you will be able to expand your empire by conquering provinces. These provinces will become part of your empire and another responsibility of yours.
  • Each turn you will be able to view a menu of options corresponding to a number. You will input a number which will show you graphs of your army size, morale, wealth, and a map of your current empire.
  • On age: You will live from 18 to 100 years old. The older you are, the higher your score.
  • On wealth: You will earn anywhere from 0 to 100 points.
  • On empire size: There are 16 provinces. Your score will be determined by multiplying the number of provinces you have captured by 6. This allows for a range of 0 to 96 for your score.
  • On morale: Morale is dependent on the happiness of your subjects. This will be scored on a scale of 0 to 100. Remember to keep your people happy to prevent possible rebellions!

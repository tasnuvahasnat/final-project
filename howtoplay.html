MONARCHY MADNESS

Stuy Students against Yellow Minions (SSAYM), 2021

Rules of the Game

How to Play

  • Events will occur annually and offer you a chance to choose an option in response to an event. Your decision will impact your army size, morale, or wealth. You will be given one event each turn.
  • Depending on the choices you make, you will be able to expand your empire by conquering provinces. These provinces will become part of your empire and another responsibility of yours.
  • Each turn you will be able to view a menu of options corresponding to a number. You will input a number which will either show you a military menu, allow you to manage your provinces, or show you graphs of your army size, morale, and wealth. You may also input a number to proceed to the next turn.

Status Changes

  • On age: You will live from 18 to 100 years old. The older you are, the higher your score. You may die in response to an event and lose the game; so, choose wisely.
  • On wealth: You begin with 30 wealth points. If you reach below 5 wealth points, your empire will collapse and you will lose.
  • On empire size/provinces: There are 16 provinces. Your score will be determined by multiplying the number of provinces you have captured by 6. This allows for a range of 0 to 96 for your score. You will be able to claim provinces using your free troops, these will be able to move around throughout the empire. The game starts with 3 free troops. Your competitors will have provinces that are green and red. Your provinces will be blue.
  • On military troops: You begin with 100 troops situated throughout your empire. You will be given chances to move your troops and view how many troops per province through the province management menu.
  • On morale: Morale is dependent on the happiness of your subjects. Your people begin with a morale of 70. If this number goes below 10, the likelihood of rebellions will increase. A rebellion may lead to you being overthrown and replaced. So, remember to keep your people happy.

About The Game:

Only You Decide Your Fate

Monarchy Madness allows you to live out your fantasies of governing an empire. You will be given various situations to respond to each year. Try to make the best decisions for the empire You can use your troops to conquer various provinces and gain wealth that will bring prosperity to your empire. You will also be tasked with keeping the morale of your people high. Watch out for devastating rebellions! As you conquer more provinces, your army will encounter more obstacles. Remember to not overexert your army, or else you may not have many soldiers left. The longer you live, the higher your winning score!

Features

  • Choose your empire and character.
  • Collect up to 16 provinces.
  • Fight enemy troops.
  • Hundreds of different possibilities of both failure and success!
